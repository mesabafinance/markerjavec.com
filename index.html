About Mark

For 25 years, Mark has developed unique skills to observe, interpret, and execute decisive strategies in agribusiness. His expertise has resulted in over 1000 transactions facilitated in a diverse range of countries, across many commodity types, and targeted end-user markets on several continents.

Today, Mark specializes in funding and developing agribusinesses around the world. The projects he invest in cover a wide range of technologies, including agricultural drone technology, indoor farming, and financing of agricultural equipment for farmers across the United States and Europe.
